Enugu

Enugu is a state in eastern Nigeria. It is bordered by Kogi and Benue to the North, Abia and Imo to the south, Ebonyi to the east and Anambra to the west. The state is a center of innovation, technology, education, business, and tourist attraction/entertainment. Enugu is known for it's historic coal mining, thus, the nickname 'Coal City.' It is also known as the capital state of the Igbo nation (a prestigious and one of the most largest ethnicity in Africa), who are majorly christians. Enugu city, the state capital is a bustling city with lots of greenery to give the residents a sense of closeness to nature and modern infrastructure to give the thrill of city life.
